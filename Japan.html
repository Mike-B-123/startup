Japan

Japan is a beautiful and exciting country country filled with rich history and culture! Some fun facts about Japan:
Japan is actually made up of 6,582 islands, but only about 430 are inhabited. The largest islands are Kyushu, Hokkaido, Shikoku, and Honshu.
Japanese is known for their high speed "bullet trains" and their punctuality, with an average delay of only 18 seconds. Try riding one next time you go!

Atlas's Recommendations
